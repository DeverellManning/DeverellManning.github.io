What is Feltrush Laundry?

Feltrush Laundry is my website for presenting my creative output in a place I can control.

What stuff is on here?

  • Art - Traditional and Digital Drawing.
  • Writing - Short Stories, Essays, and even Room Descriptions!
  • Code - P5.JS sketches
  • Papers - The Majority of my work.
  • My projects, such as EligoTextum/HOUSE.
  • Resources and links.
  • Info about How I made this site.
  • Maybe clubs/ societies info.
  • Basically, things I (and my friends) are interested in.

A brief history of Feltrush Laundry

In October of 2020, I started my website as DeverellManning.github.io. I found Github Pages as a free web host. That's when I made my github account. Four years, that was all I used Github for. In addition, my knowledge of Web Development and Servers was at a -- beginers level. Soon, I realized that Github pages had limitations. Like, it's just a web server. I don't have access to it, just a static website in a git repo. That was all fine at the time, but eventually I wanted to move.

During those 4 years, I learned many things. This website went through several iterations. It now looks fancier than ever. Although I wrote a great style sheet, it has been seriously lacking in content. Prior to the move to xepher.net, I spent a week redesigning the entire site. In order to solve my content problem, I decided I needed to do two things. One, open up my site to a much larger vareity of content, and Two, creating a system to quickly create new pages with out spending so much time manging everything. That pretty much brings us up to date, I'm looking forward to the move!
