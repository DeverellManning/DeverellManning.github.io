These Are Papers

I'm back. What you clicked on brought you here. I've been doing a bunch of stuff, but I realize that it's just to hard to put it on my webite. So, I made papers! Using this format, it's easier to publish content. These papers are managed by a bunch of bash scripts I'm writing. I can run a script to make a new paper, and all of the templating and indexes are taken care of. Then I can just write the content. The papers are all linked to eachother, in the topbar, so you can read them chronologically. Later, I will also add other indexes, like my gallery.

Qualifications for a Paper

To ensure that each paper is worth reading, I'm have a minimum standard for new papers. To write a new paper, it has to have at least a paragraph of text, or a large picture with a description. Most papers won't have much side content, and will mainly be on one topic. For papers presenting my art, they only need the picture and a description. The point of all of this is that I can present my projects on a website with out having to spend hours maintaining it. Basically minimal maintenance and maximum ease of content creation.
